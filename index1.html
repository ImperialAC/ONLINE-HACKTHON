Main Course: Halloween Foods

Halloween is not just about costumes and decorations; it's also a time for deliciously spooky foods! Here are some popular treats:

  • Pumpkin Soup: A warm and comforting dish, often spiced with cinnamon and nutmeg.
  • Witch's Brew Punch: A fun, fizzy drink made with green punch and floating gummy worms.
  • Monster Eyeballs: Meatballs topped with mozzarella cheese and an olive to resemble creepy eyeballs.
  • Ghostly Cupcakes: Vanilla cupcakes decorated with white frosting and chocolate chip eyes to look like little ghosts.
  • Candy Corn Treats: A mix of candy corn, nuts, and popcorn for a sweet and salty snack.
  • Spider Web Dip: A layered dip with sour cream piped on top to create a spider web effect.
  • Graveyard Dirt Cups: Chocolate pudding cups topped with crushed Oreos and gummy worms for a spooky dessert!
